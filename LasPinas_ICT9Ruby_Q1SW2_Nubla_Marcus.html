Noli Me Tangere Characters

Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin
A well educated and wealthy man who studied in Europe for 7 years and just got back to the Philippines. Said to be sophisticated, highly esteemed, very idealistic, and have high connections.
María Clara
A high social standing woman with a complicated family relationship with her biological father. She wanted to marry Ibarra (Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin), but her father, Father Dámaso, disproved of it.
Father Dámaso
Father of María Clara, is a power hungry and corrupt spanshis preist whose lived with Native Filipinos. He is a racist who wants to ruin the lives of everyone he doesnt like.
Elías
A rouge man who dislikes the Spanish and the Catholic churches. He works with Ibarra (Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin) with the bad things happening at the moment.
Father Salví
A more cunning and dangerous priest than Father Dámaso. He uses his power and strategy to influence people's religion and political views.
